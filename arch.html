Architecture

Architecture is one of the fields in which I am particularly interested, as I have spent a lot of time studying and working on it.

In Thailand, I studied architecture for five years before working in the field for another three years. It was a lot of fun and excitement at the time because everything was trial and error. I had a chance to explore new things, face new challenges, adapt to new skills, gain new knowledge, and learn how to deal with work stress.

The best part of working in the field is that I had the chance to explore new architecture and gain new techniques. When visiting places with good design, I not only get to learn about the concept of the building but also about space planning, room arrangement, construction detail, and more.
